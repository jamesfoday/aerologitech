About us

Aerologictech Limited supports companies and individuals by constantly striving to offer innovative solutions that make their air and travels easier and increase their efficiency and productivity.

Aerologictech Limited offers a tailor-made welcome and support service at Banjul International Airport. We ensure comfort and serenity during guests’ arrival or departure at the airport.

Journey becomes an easy, fast and hassle-free experience with our hostesses. Our efforts are primarily focused on helping to bypass long lines, remove language barriers and make passing through the airport easy. All the most minor needs are anticipated, and all the formalities related to the travel become a moment of pleasure.

Whether it is arrival or departure at the Banjul International Airport, the expertise and rigour of our hostesses will transform guests’ difficulties into solutions. Recruited according to criteria specific to our business, they provide every guest with excellent service. Smiling, discreet and efficient, they welcome you and accompany you 365 days a year and 24 hours a day.

Aerologictech Limited enabling development on the international scene. Thanks to this cooperation, fostered by a shared vision of the future. The two companies are helping to structure the Gambian scene in the global security and services industry.
